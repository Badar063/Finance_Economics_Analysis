Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of financial markets and economic indicators across multiple dimensions, including stock volatility, personal spending behavior, portfolio performance, macroeconomic correlations and cryptocurrency trends.
Datasets


stock_prices.csv – Daily stock prices for 10 companies, including volatility measures


personal_spending.csv – Personal spending by category over a 24-month period


portfolio_performance.csv – Investment portfolio performance compared with benchmark indices over 36 months


economic_indicators.csv – Key economic indicators and unemployment rates over 48 months


cryptocurrency.csv – Cryptocurrency prices and trading volumes over 50 days


Analysis Objectives


Monitor stock price movements and compute volatility metrics


Evaluate personal spending trends across categories over time


Assess portfolio performance relative to market benchmarks


Analyze correlations between economic indicators and unemployment rates

Key Features of This Project:
1. Stock Volatility Analysis
Calculates daily and annualized volatility

Measures maximum drawdown

Computes Sharpe ratios

Visualizes stock price movements and risk metrics

2. Personal Spending Analysis
Tracks spending by category over time

Compares actual spending vs budget

Identifies spending patterns and trends

Visualizes spending distribution and trends

3. Portfolio Performance Analysis
Compares portfolio returns against benchmarks

Calculates risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe Ratio)

Measures excess returns and tracking error

Analyzes drawdowns and recovery periods

4. Economic Indicators Analysis
Studies correlations between economic variables

Analyzes leading indicators for unemployment

Visualizes economic cycles and relationships

Performs statistical analysis of economic trends

5. Cryptocurrency Analysis
Tracks price trends and trading volumes

Analyzes risk-return profiles

Studies volume-price relationships

Compares performance across different cryptocurrencies

Generated Outputs:
5 PNG files with comprehensive visualizations

Summary report in text format

All datasets in CSV format

Correlation matrices and statistical results


Investigate cryptocurrency price dynamics and trading volume patterns
